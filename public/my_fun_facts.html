My Favorite Book

Of Mice and Men - John Steinbeck

For someone that doesn't read books often, I enjoyed Of Mice and Men. I read this book in high school and out of all of the books I was forced to read in high school this one I enjoyed the most. It's a touching story about two men, one with a mental disability that is trying to find work. It starts with the two men being close friends but ends with Lennie (the mentally disabled character) dying for accidentally murdering an innocent woman.
